Steps to Get Electricity at Home

  • Search electrician.
  • Submit an application form online or in-person at any LEC office (Test Application).
  • Provide proof of residence (e.g. a lease agreement or utility bill) (Test Application).
  • Schedule a site inspection with an LEC technician.
  • Receive a quote for installation costs and make payment.
  • After payment, installation will be scheduled within 7 working days.
  • Once installation is complete, electricity will be activated at your residence.
